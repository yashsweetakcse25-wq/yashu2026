About Dr. B. R. Ambedkar

Dr. Bhimrao Ramji Ambedkar was born on 14th April 1891. He was a great social reformer, economist, jurist, and the chief architect of the Indian Constitution. He dedicated his life to fighting against social discrimination and worked for equality and justice. Dr. Ambedkar played a key role in shaping modern India and promoting education and human rights. He is widely respected for his contributions to society and nation-building.

Achievements

  • Chief Architect of the Indian Constitution
  • First Law Minister of Independent India
  • Champion of Social Justice and Equality
  • Established Reserve Bank of India concept through his research
  • Founded Bahishkrit Hitakarini Sabha
  • Awarded Bharat Ratna (1990)
